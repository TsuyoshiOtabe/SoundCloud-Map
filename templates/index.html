Tracks on a Map

Navigate the world's music

With Tracks on a Map you can ...

  • listen to the recent tracks on SoundCloud
  • choose music from around the world
  • filter tracks by genre
  • listen to the sound of the cities
  • just keep playing; continuous random playback included
  • skip to the next track, if you don't like the current

Built by Johan Uhle with the awesome help of
Katharina Birkenbach, Eric Wahlforss and Henrik Berggren.

Major parts have been written at the Music Hack Day Berlin in autumn 2009.

If you want to learn more about the project, check the repository on GitHub.
