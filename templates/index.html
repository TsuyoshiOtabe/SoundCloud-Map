Tracks on a Map

Listen To The World's Music

On Tracks on a Map you can listen to SoundCloud tracks from around the world from any genre. Just click a city to start the fun!

Built by Johan Uhle with the awesome help of
Katharina Birkenbach, Eric Wahlforss and Henrik Berggren.

Major parts have been written at the Music Hack Day Berlin in autumn 2009.

If you want to learn more about the project, follow us on Twitter and check the repository on GitHub.
